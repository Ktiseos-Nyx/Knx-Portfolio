# Welcome to Ktiseos Nyx

We're a small creative collective building open-source AI art models — checkpoints, LoRAs, embeddings, and whatever else we feel like experimenting with.

## Who We Are

Ktiseos Nyx is spearheaded by **0FTh3N1GHT** and overseen by **Earth&Dusk Media**. Our core team:

- **Duskfallcrew** — The main force behind most of our models
- **Earthnicity** — Landscape and environment specialist
- **OtnAngel** — Character and style explorer

## What We Do

We train and release models for the Stable Diffusion ecosystem — SDXL, Pony, Flux, you name it. Everything we release is **free and open source**.

Our models live primarily on [Civitai](https://civitai.com) and [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co), and this site serves as our central hub to browse everything in one place.

## Why Open Source?

Because gatekeeping art tools is boring. We believe in community-driven creation — the more people who have access to good models, the more interesting art gets made.

Stay tuned for more posts about our process, new releases, and the occasional tutorial.
